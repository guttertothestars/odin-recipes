The Toolbox Screwdriver

Vodka Cocltail

This is a slight twist on the classic orange juice and vodka Screwdriver. With the addition of Cointreau and cranberry juice, it's a little easier to get hammered.

A splash of red cranberry juice gives this traditional cocktail slightly more depth.

Ingredients

  • 3 oz orange juice pulp free
  • 2 oz vodka
  • 1 oz cranberry juice
  • orange peel for garnish

Instructions

Pour together juices and vodka. Garnish with the orange peel and enjoy!
